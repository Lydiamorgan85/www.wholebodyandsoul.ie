Monthly Women's Circle

Sacred space for women to gather, heal, and rise together

Last Sunday of Every Month

We gather on the final Sunday of each month to close one cycle and prepare for the next. This isn't just a workshop—it's a ritual, a homecoming, a sacred pause before stepping into a new month with intention and clarity.

By meeting at the threshold between months, we honor the natural rhythm of endings and beginnings. We release what no longer serves us and set our frequency for the month ahead. When you tend to yourself at this potent time, you carry that energy forward—impacting your whole body and soul throughout the weeks to come.

The 3-Hour Journey

This is not a rushed experience. We take our time. We breathe. We feel. We nourish. Three hours to remember who you are beneath all the doing, all the roles, all the noise.

Opening Circle

We begin by creating sacred space together. Introductions, intentions, and the weaving of our individual energies into a collective container of trust and safety.

Grounding Practice

Before we can go deep, we must arrive. Gentle practices to bring you fully into your body, into the present moment, into this circle.

Shadow Work

Lydia guides us into the depths—exploring what we've kept hidden, what we've rejected, what's asking to be seen and integrated with compassion.

Conscious Breathwork

Together we breathe. We release. We activate. Breathwork to move what's stuck, to open what's closed, to remember the power of our own life force.

Sound Bath Journey

Miya bathes us in healing frequencies. Singing bowls, voice, and sacred sound to soothe your nervous system and guide you into profound rest and integration.

Grounding & Journaling

We return gently. Time to write, to reflect, to capture what wants to be remembered. Integration through the written word and quiet contemplation.

Magickal Herbal Tea

Homemade herbal blends crafted with intention. Sip slowly. Let the plants support your system as you settle back into your body.

Organic Nourishment

A full organic meal—meat-free, allergen-free, and packed with flavor. Food as medicine. Food as love. We eat together in sacred communion.

Closing Circle

We complete the circle as we began—together. Reflections, gratitude, and the sealing of our collective experience before we return to the world.

Why the Last Sunday?

There's power in timing. The final Sunday of each month sits at a threshold—one foot in what was, one foot in what's coming. This is liminal space, where transformation happens.

When you tend to yourself at this transition point, you're not just healing—you're setting your energetic frequency for the entire month ahead. You're choosing how you want to feel, how you want to show up, what energy you want to carry forward.

By making this a monthly ritual, you create a rhythm. Your body learns. Your nervous system anticipates this sacred pause. Month by month, you build a foundation of self-tending that compounds, that deepens, that becomes who you are.

Start each month from a place of wholeness, clarity, and elevated frequency. Your whole body and soul will thank you.
