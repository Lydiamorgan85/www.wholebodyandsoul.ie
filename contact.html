What to Include in Your Message

To help us respond to you as quickly and helpfully as possible, please include:

For Workshop Bookings: Your name, which workshop you're interested in, preferred dates if you have them, and any questions about what to expect.

For Women's Circle: Your name, which month's circle you'd like to join, and whether you have any dietary requirements we should know about beyond our standard allergen-free, meat-free menu.

For General Inquiries: Whatever's on your heart. We're here to answer questions, provide clarity, and support you in finding the right offering for where you are in your journey.
